Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 5, 2026

Introduction

Parking Tracker ("we," "our," or "us") operates the parking alert notification service at parking.foxjason.com. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our service.

By using Parking Tracker, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you subscribe to our service:

  • Phone Number: Your mobile phone number, used to send SMS alerts about parking availability.
  • Subscription Preferences: The ski resorts and dates you choose to monitor for parking availability.
  • Invitation Code: The invitation code you used to register (for first-time users).

We do not collect your name, email address, payment information, or any other personal identifiers beyond what is listed above.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely for the following purposes:

  • SMS Notifications: To send you text message alerts when parking becomes available at your selected ski resorts on your chosen dates.
  • Service Management: To manage your subscriptions, including processing unsubscribe requests.
  • Service Improvement: To monitor and improve the reliability and performance of our service.

We do not use your information for marketing purposes, and we will never send you promotional messages unrelated to your parking alerts.

SMS Messaging

By providing your phone number and subscribing to alerts, you expressly consent to receive automated SMS text messages from Parking Tracker regarding parking availability.

  • Message Frequency: Message frequency varies based on parking availability changes. You may receive multiple messages per day during active monitoring periods.
  • Message Content: Messages will contain parking availability status updates for the resorts and dates you selected.
  • Carrier Charges: Standard message and data rates may apply. Check with your mobile carrier for details about your plan.
  • Opt-Out: You can stop receiving messages at any time by replying STOP to any message, clicking the unsubscribe link in any SMS, or visiting our unsubscribe page.
  • Help: Reply HELP to any message for assistance, or email support@foxjason.com.

Data Storage and Security

Your information is stored securely using Amazon Web Services (AWS) infrastructure, including encrypted databases and secure access controls. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Subscription data is automatically deleted after the selected date has passed. Phone registry information is retained until you unsubscribe from all alerts or request deletion.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • Twilio: We use Twilio as our SMS service provider to deliver text messages to your phone. Twilio processes your phone number solely for the purpose of delivering our messages. See Twilio's Privacy Policy for more information.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or governmental authority.

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You can view your active subscriptions by using the unsubscribe link sent in your SMS messages.
  • Deletion: You can delete your subscriptions at any time through our unsubscribe page.
  • Opt-Out: You can stop receiving SMS messages at any time by unsubscribing.

To request complete deletion of all your data, please unsubscribe from all alerts using the master unsubscribe link in any SMS message.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. Your continued use of the service after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
